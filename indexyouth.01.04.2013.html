Today's youth athletes are tomorrow's champions! As the #1 high school and junior high school participatory sport, Track & Field, Cross Country, and Race walking offer a variety of competitive and fitness opportunities.

 

The Pacific Association is the largest youth program Northern California, where nearly 4,000 youth athletes compete each year in Track & Field and Cross Country. Many of America's Collegiate Athletes began as youth athletes, including stars such as Ashton Purvis, Amber Purvis, Javis Best, Jordan Hasay and Olympians such as Jeremy Wariner, Allyson Felix, Sanya Richards, and Lauryn Williams.

 

Join USATF today and make your Olympic dreams come true!
