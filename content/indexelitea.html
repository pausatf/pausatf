As of July, 2013, the Pacific Association elite athletes are represented on the Pacific Association Executive Board by elite discus thrower Jere Summers. She would like to hear from you! email: throwertrainer@gmail.com. The Pacific Association thanks Stephanie Brown Trafton for all her efforts in this role over the years.

Definition of elite athlete: an athlete who has competed or who has qualified to compete in a USA National Track & Field Championship or U.S. Olympic Team Trials.
