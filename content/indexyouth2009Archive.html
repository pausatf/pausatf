A familiar site on the track and at the XC meets: Bob Shor in full regalia		 Coach, Official, Youth XC Chair, and longtime Association member Bob Shor is on the road to recovery after being diagnosed with cancer. The Santa Rosa Coach recently underwent surgery to remove the cancer from his lung and is now at home resting and doing well.

The Pacific Association and all its members would like to wish Bob all the best for a full and speedy recovery. There is a special place in the hearts and prayers of all of your friends and family.
Cards and notes may be sent to his home address: 1730 St. Francis Rd., Santa Rosa, CA 95409.
