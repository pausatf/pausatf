2009 Pacific Association USATF Masters Track & Field Championships

 

2009 Pacific Association USATF Masters Track & Field Championships

(Open Athletes Welcome)

Saturday, June 13, 2009

At Folsom High School

SPONSORED BY THE Pacific Association

Registration Deadline Midnight, June 9, 2009

ENTRIES:        All “Entries” are online ONLY at  www.PAUSATF.com    No phone or FAX entries accepted. 

FEES:              $20 First Event, $5 thereafter, $30 Relay                     

TIMING:           Fully electronic timing (FinishLynx)                          

DIVISIONS:      Age on June 13, 2009 determines age group – 30 yrs and above in 5 year age groups

AWARDS:        Medals to first three places in all Master events by age-group   (not Open Division)

T-SHIRTS:        Custom T-Shirt For Sale at the meet

FACILITIES:     All Weather 400 meter track surface (1/4” spikes only) concrete throwing rings Long and Triple Jump will have an open pit in all age group divisions

HEATS:            We will combine races where we do not have enough runners by specific age group divisions
