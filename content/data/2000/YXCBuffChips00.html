ROCKLIN, CA  -- 10/29/00

A BIG THANKS to all of you who supported the Buffalo Chips Youth 
XC Invitational  2000 -- Runners, Parents, Coaches, Friends, Relatives,
and VOLUNTEERS!!!  With preparation time of less than two weeks,
we had nearly 100 registered youth athletes -- THANK YOU for helping
us make it happen.

There were numerous people who thanked us for presenting such a quality
event, and there is no way it would have happened without the incredible
volunteers who kept things "running" smoothly . . . . and without all of our
kids who deserve all the support we can give them in their endeavors to
perform to the best of their abilities.  

Following are the Final Results from our meet.  We look forward to seeing
you in RENO!!

Thank You,

Ken Briscoe
Buffalo Chips Youth Running Team
Sacramento, CA  Ph.(916) 456-9265
