STANDINGS

Final 2001

revised 12/8/01

  
Well, the 2000/2001 GP season is now over.  It was a long series and now we
can relax a bit and ease into the 2002 season where the season will be on a
calendar year basis, starting with the Jed Smith in February and ending with
the Quad Dipsea in November.

Congratulations to the following division champions:

Open Men                Neal Gottlieb
Senior Open Men         Scott St. John
Master Men              Michael Sweeney
Senior Men              Charles Crompton
Super Senior Men        Lee Rhodes
Veteran Men             Richard Laine

Open Women              Jennifer Devine-Pfeifer
Senior Open Women       Rena Schumann
Master Women            Laura Kulsik
Senior Women            Kathryn Forshey-Trabert
Super Senior Women      Margaret Curtis
Veteran Women           Helen Klein

Mens Team       Tamalpa Runners
Womens Team     Bay Area Ultra Runners
Mixed Team      Silver State Striders
Team Total      Bay Area Ultra Runners

This mail has several parts:
The final individual standings
The final team standings
The points scored at the Quad Dipsea
The points scored at the Helen Klein 50K
The points scored at the Helen Klein 50 Mile
The tentative schedule for 2002.  There are still some event dates that are
being reviewed and one or two events are still being considered.

So far we haven't received any major concerns on the new rules that were 
implemented in this past series.  Unless we hear from you we assume that 
you are satisfied with the current format.  Let us know if it is otherwise.

Also, a reminder to renew your PA/USATF membership if you haven't already 
done so.  Unfortunately renewal notices aren't sent out.

The next GP event is the Jed Smith on February 9th.
Hopefully these standings are error free and the next update won't be till 
after the Jed Smith.  If we have some prize money to distribute as we have
had in the past few years I'll send out an update on that.  And also when 
we get a firmed up schedule.

Have a happy holidays and best wishes for the new year.
Take care and hopefully I'll see you on the trails.
