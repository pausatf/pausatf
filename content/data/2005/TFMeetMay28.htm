2005 PA-USATF Open T & F Championships

May 28th– Hughes Stadium

Sacramento City College

Entry Form

Officials Assignments

 

Facilities:                    Hughes Stadium, on the campus of Sacramento City College.

(9) nine lane Atlas 5000 surface throughout, including steeplechase, javelin approaches and all runways. Required spike length is ¼ inch. The throwing rings are brushed concrete.

 

Eligibility:                   All competitors must be a registered USATF Athlete. Only collegiate athletes who compete for their institutions and are registered by their coaches are not required to be USATF member. USATF membership cards are available for $20.00 by contacting the Pacific Association Office at (916) 983-4715, or you may download an application on-line from www.pausatf.org.  Memberships will also be on sale the day of the meet.

 

Entry Fees:                 PREREGISTRATION: $15.00 per event if entry is postmarked by Monday, May 16, 2005, with fees payable to PAUSATF.  No telephone or fax entries.

MEET DAY REGISTRATION: $20.00 per event.  The late entry deadline is 90 minutes prior to the scheduled event time.    NO EXCEPTIONS.

 

Mail Entry                  Mail all entry forms to Lisa Bauduin, Women’s Track & Field, Sacramento

Form to:                      City College 3835 Freeport Boulevard   Sacramento, CA 95822-1386.  Checks must be made payable to PAUSATF.

 

Event                          Registered track contestants check-in with the running clerk at least 30 minutes prior

Check-In:                   to the event start time.  All athletes must check in at registration table before competition.  Field events should check in 45 minutes prior to event start at event site.

 

Heats/Flights              All running events will be run as finals only with seeding based

Organization:              on submitted marks.  Field event competitors will have three initial attempts 

With the top (9) nine competitors qualifying for the finals. Weights and measures will be from 9:00 - 12:30 p.m.

 

Further                        For further information, contact the SCC Women’s Track & Field Office at

Information:               (916) 558-2610, email: bauduil@scc.losrios.edu or the Pacific Association Office at (916) 983-4715, www.pausatf.org.

 

PAUSATF                   Earn double points towards prize monies being awarded by the Pacific

Grand Prix:                 Association Open T & F Committee.  To register, you must be a current PA-USATF member. The Pacific Association Grand Prix is a series, culminating with these Association Championships.  $15,000.00 will be awarded to the association’s top ten men and top ten women utilizing the 2001 IAAF Scoring Table.  Contact Open T & F Chair Jerry Colman at (916) 487-3175, or e-mail: sactc@aol.com for further information and to register.
