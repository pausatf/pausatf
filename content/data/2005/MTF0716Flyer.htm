2005 USATF Pacific Association Masters Track & Field Championship

(Open Athletes Welcome)

Saturday, July 16, 2005

At Los Gatos High School, 20 High School Court, Los Gatos, CA 95030

SPONSORED BY LOS GATOS ATHLETIC ASSOCIATION

Pre-Registration Deadline Tuesday, July 7, 2005

 

ENTRIES:         All Pre-Registration entries must be accompanied by Signed Waiver, Entry Form and Fees for all events you wish to enter.  No phone or FAX entries accepted.  You can enter on the day of the meet but you must pay a late entry fee.  If any questions call Joy Upshaw-Margerum at 510.816.8009 or email:  coachjoy@sbcglobal.net

 

FEES:               Prior to July 7th  or Envelope Postmarked       $20 First Event, $5 thereafter, $30 Relay

                                Late Entry Fees (after July 7th)                           $30 First Event, $10 thereafter, $40 Relay

 

TIMING:            Fully electronic timing (FinishLynx)                          

 

DIVISIONS:      Age on July 16, 2005 determines age group – 30 yrs and above in 5 year age groups

 

AWARDS:         Medals to first three places in all Masters events by age-group   (not Open Division)

 

T-SHIRTS:        Custom T-Shirt For Sale at the meet

 

FACILITIES:     New All Weather 400 meter track surface (1/4” spikes only) concrete throwing rings Long and Triple Jump will have an open pit in all age group divisions

 

HEATS:            We will combine races where we do not have enough runners by specific age group divisions
