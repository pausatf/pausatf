Olympian Week: Jan.29-Feb.3 2007


Events and Clinic Information

Here is some information about Olympian Week-Jan.29-Feb.3 2007.
There will many Olympians here in the area that week and many great events.

Willie Davenport Celebrity Golf Tournament
Metropolitan Golf Links-Oakland
February 2, 2007
11:30 Start
Shot Gun Start/ 4 Player Scramble
$150 per person-Includes golf, cart, tee prizes, goodie bag, buffet lunch
Awards to teams, closet to the pin, longest drive
