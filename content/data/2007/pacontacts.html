Pacific Association Board of Athletics/Committee Chairs

The Pacific Association of USA Track & Field is governed by a Board of Athletics, which in turn is governed by the national by-Laws of USA Track & Field, and its own Association by-Laws.

The Board of Athletics consists of the Association officers, immediate past president, chairpersons of each of the Association's committees, and elected athletes and club representatives. The Board of Athletics meets approximately every other month (currently alternating locations between Sacramento and Foster City, CA). All PA/USATF members are invited to attend Board meetings PAUSATF meeting dates and times.
