Dearest Family and Friends,

Paff's passing on July 9th has left a huge hole in all our hearts.

 Please join us for a memorial of thanksgiving for the life of Ralph S. Paffenbarger, Jr., M.D. to be held:

SATURDAY,  OCTOBER 6th
11:00 A.M.
STANFORD FACULTY CLUB
STANFORD, CA    94305

Details of the Faculty Club, plus directions, can be found on the Stanford University website: www.stanford.edu/group/facultyclub

For our out of town guests we suggest the following accommodations:

625 El Camino Real
Palo Alto, CA.     Phone (650) 328-2800

Stanford Terrace Inn
531 Stanford Avenue
Palo Alto, CA.     Phone  (650) 857-0333

The duration of the memorial will be approximately 75 minutes, with a buffet luncheon, following, in the Faculty Club courtyard.
We plan to videotape the service for posterity.  Also, we will be  compiling a memory book for our grandchildren.  If you are inclined,  please jot down your favorite anecdote, or include a photo of Paff,  and mail this to me.

I would like to thank each of you for your outpouring of love and support during this past month, and I look forward to your being with us on October 6th.

Warmly,
