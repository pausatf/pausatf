Club News and Notes: March 23, 2007

Club T&F Championships return to NYC

The 2007 USATF National Club Track&Field Championships will be held at New York City's Icahn Stadium on July 13-14. The meet will be conducted by the USA Track & Field Metropolitan Association, in cooperation with an outstanding coalition of local USATF Elite Development track clubs such as the Nike Central Park Track Club, Executive Track Club, and Shore Athletic Club of New Jersey. Meet information is available on the USATF website at www.usatf.org/events/2007/USATFClubTFChampionships/
