Cal Hosts Don Bowden Dinner April 26

BERKELEY - The California track and field program will host a dinner honoring former Golden Bears great Don Bowden on Thursday, April 26, at the Doubletree Hotel in Berkeley. The Cal Track and Field Steering Committee is sponsoring the dinner in honor of the 50th anniversary of Bowden becoming the first American to run a sub-four-minute mile.

The dinner costs $75 per person and $1,000 per table of 10, which includes a donation towards the Cal scholarship fund.

If you are interested in reserving a room at the Doubletree Hotel at a special rate of $119 plus tax, please contact the hotel directly at 510-548-7920 and reference the "Cal Don Bowden Dinner." Reservations at this rate must be made by March 26. After that, a special Cal discount of 10% off the normal rate will apply.

For reservations or more information, call Barbara Oseroff at 510-643-7246.
