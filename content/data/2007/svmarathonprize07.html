The Prize Purse for The 2007 Metro Silicon Valley Marathon, Half-Marathon presented by Brooks, and 5K Run/Walk presented by Silicon Valley Community Newspapers has been announced! On top of the new prizes, the course is fast and the weather generally cooperates. This is not the year to miss the 10th annual Silicon Valley tradition.
