Pacific Association Live-scan Background Checks

Live-scan Background Check Services will be available at the 2007 Youth Track and Field Meets at the Santa Rosa Meet on March 31. Check the Youth Track & Field Schedule for meet information.

Important! The scans will only be performed at 8 a.m. - 11 a.m.!

Coaches, volunteers, and officials requiring the checks will need to bring:

  • Their 2007 USATF Card or Number
  • A valid identification (CA driver's license)
  • A pen to complete the form.

    The process takes anywhere from 10 - 20 minutes depending on the length of the lines.
