1999 PA-USATF Open T&F Championship Schedule
29 May, 1999 - Modesto Junior College Stadium

ENTRY FORM   (print out and mail in)


You're invited to one of America's most venerable Track and Field venues - 
the Modesto JC stadium: home of 32 world records, for the 1999 edition of 
the PA-USATF Open Men's and Women's Championships to be held the evening of 
Saturday, 29 May

Facilities:  Fast nine lane polyurethane track with all-weather steeple 
approach and return. 1/4 inch spike required.  Accutrac timing for all races. 
The throwing rings are bushed concrete,while thejavelin runway is manicured 
grass.
Eligibility:  All competitors must be a registered USATF Athlete. Only 
collegiate athletes who compete for their institutions and are registered 
by their coaches are not required to be USATF members. USATF membership 
card are available for $15.00 by contacting the PA Office at 916.983-4715 
or you may download an application on-line from www.pausatf.org.  Memberships 
will also be on sale the day ofthe meet with updated membership lists to 
check membership status.
Entry Fee: preregistration $7.00 per event if entry is postmarked by 
Saturday, 22 May, l999, with fees payable to M.A.C.  Meet day registration - 
$10.00 per event. The late entry deadline is 30 minutes prior to the scheduled 
event time...no exceptions.  Meet day registation will be processed from the
press box in the west stands - section 'C'.
Mail entry form to: Amy Grafius, MJC Track & Field Office, Modesto JC 
Athletics, Modesto, CA  95350-5800. Entries will not accepted after deadline 
date.
Pacific Association Grand Prix: Earn double points towards monies being 
awarded by the Pacific Association Open T&F Committee; To register, you must 
be a Pacific Association resident and  current PAUSTF member. The Pacific 
Association Grand Prix is a foru-meet format series, culminating with these 
Association Championships. $15,000.00 provided by the Series Sponsor: Shooting
Star Publications, will be awarded to the association's top twelve men and 
twelve women utilizing the Mercier International Scoring Table. Contact Open
T&F Chair Jerry Colman at 916.487-3175, or e-mail: sactc@aol.com for further 
information and to register.
Championships Awards: First place: attractive USATF Championship t-shirts. 
Second and third places: USATF Championship medals.
Event check-in: Registered track contestants check-in with the clerk at the 
starting line 15 minutes prior to the event starting time. Registered field 
contestants check-in directly at the event site. 
Heats/Flight Organization: All running events will be run as finals only 
with seeding based on submitted marks.  Field event contestants will have 
three initial efforts with the top six contestants receiving three additional
 attempts.
Concessions: Souvenir USATF t-shirts will be availabte for $12.00 each from 
the concession stand and  'Omnilite' ceramic spikes will also be on sale for 
8.00 for a bag of twenty.
Directions:  Modesto JC Stadium is located 95 miles directly east from San 
Francisco in the northern Joaquin Valley.  From Hwy. 99, exit at Kansas Ave. 
and travel east, following signs to the MJC East Campus at the corner of Tully 
Road and Coldwell.
 Further infonnation: Contact the MJC Trsck and Field Office: 209. 575-6279.
http://www.mjc.yosemite.cc.ca.us/mjcathletics. or the Pacific Association Office: 
916.983-4715, http://www.pausatf.org
