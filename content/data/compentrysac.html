From Rich Hanna, Race Director, Hanson McClain Run to Feed the Hungry
Complimentary Entry & Hotel Accommodation Offer
to Qualified PA/USATF Grand Prix Athletes

Hanson McClain Run to Feed the Hungry 10k

Sacramento Thanksgiving Morning, November 24

You are invited to spend Thanksgiving morning at one of Northern California's biggest and fastest 10k road races, The 12th Annual Hanson McClain Run to Feed the Hungry in Sacramento. The event features over $5,000 in guaranteed prize money and $2,500 in bonus money for race record and city of Sacramento 10k record performances. Prize money for open runners goes five deep: 1st - $1,000, 2nd ? $600, 3rd ? $400, 4th - $300, and 5th - $200. Masters money runs three deep: $250, $150, and $100 respectively. We have reserved a limited number of complimentary entries and hotel accommodations at the Hyatt Regency Sacramento for qualified elite runners.

To be considered, you must have run a certified 10k under 30:30 (men) and 35:00 (women) or a 5k under 14:45 (men) and 17:00 (women) within the past year.

Alternatively, the top five men and women open finishers at the PAUSTF X-Country Championships held on November 6th also qualify for the aforementioned race perks.

If interested, please contact our elite athlete coordinator George Parrott at gparrott@csus.edu or (916) 488-6580. Please contact George no later than Monday, November 21st so we can alert the media of your entry. We hope you can join us for a very memorable Thanksgiving morning in Sacramento.
