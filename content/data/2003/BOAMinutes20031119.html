and L proposals.
o "Cross Association Penalties" is a proposal submitted by the PA to
close a loophole in membership rules that allows a member who has been
banned from one active association to acquire a membership in a different
active association.

BY-LAW AMENDMENTS
o Approved unanimously the changes needed for our grievance procedure per National office.
o Majority approved (2 abstentions) for grievance hearing panels that if there are no volunteers there will be a random drawing for 3 members and two alternates from the Board members.

SAFETY ISSUES
o Sanctions require that all events have specific requirements to address safety issues.
o Events all receive these requirements in writing and by becoming
sanctioned agree to provide the requirements.
o Need to have a procedure in the PA by-laws concerning how to deal with
noncompliance.
o Motion approved unanimously that events suspicious of safety noncompliance will be reviewed on a case by case basis first by the Executive Board and then if necessary by the PA/USATF Board of Athletics.

BOARD MEETING LOCATION
o Dick Connors suggested we look into a permanent site for the Board meetings and also look at holding board meetings on the weekends. Discussion was held and it was agreed to not change the present system.
