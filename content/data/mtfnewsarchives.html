  • PA Masters Women + One Set 4x400 Relay Indoors World Record

    (l to r) Theresa Brown (45), Debbie Zakerski (42), Mary Woo (43), and Kathleen Shook (43)
    		 Three members of the Brooks Fleet Feet Team of Sacramento (Mary Woo, Theresa Brown, and Debbie Zakerski), coached by Randy Sturgeon, with a fourth member from Indiana (Kathleen Shook), sped their way to an American and World Record 4x400 meter time of 4:31.15 at the National Masters Championship Meet at Nampa, Idaho on March 13, 2005. Their splits were: Shook - 1:03.69, Woo - 1:10.26, Brown - 1:10.41, Zakerski - 1:06.63. The previous record of 4:33.14 was set in 2004 by Team USA Boston.
