Schedule Updates to Region 14 Meet

July 11-13, 2008

   

 

After careful review of our previous Junior Olympic Championships meet June 20-22, we have decided to revise the time schedule for the upcoming Region 14 Track and Field meet on July 11-13 at Granada High School in Livermore.  On Saturday, July 12 the regular javelin will start immediately after the Midget and Bantam age group mini javelin with an approximate start time of 12 noon.  Most running events are now scheduled for an earlier start time. Please advise your parents and athletes of the new scheduled times. 

There are no changes to the schedule on Friday, July 11th at Laney College.
