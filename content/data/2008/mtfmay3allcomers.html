All-Comers Meet at Skyline High School

May 3, 2008

Mills  College is hosting a meet this Saturday, May 3, 2008 at Skyline High School . They are encouraging masters’ athletes to enter.  

All events are free and open to the public. Times will be handheld. Results will be posted on the  Mills College website by Monday, May 5th/

 

Schedule of Events (all events are open and coed):

9:00 Registration begins

10:00 1500m

10:10 Javelin

10:30 100m

11:00 Discus

11:15 Long jump

12:00 Shot put

12:30 4 x 400m relay
