Julia Bounds of the Palo Alto Lightning Track Club takes First Place at the 2008 National Junior Olympic Cross Country Championships

 
On Saturday, December 13, 2008, at Pole Green Park, in Richmond ,VA, Palo Alto Lightning, provided one of two PA athletes that took home a first place age group medal.

The top 25 finishers in each age group were awarded medals and "All American" honors during the closing ceremonies.

The cross country National Junior Olympics featured the top 20 finishers from each of the 15 regions across the country..."the best of the best."

In Julia's Bantam Girl age group, 220 girls (age 10 or younger) raced the 3K course in the cold conditions.  Julia trained hard and finished her undefeated season as a Champion.  Good Job!
