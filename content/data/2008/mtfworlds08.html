2008 World Masters Indoors T&F Top Marks by Californians

The number after the athletes' age group is their place in that event. Those noted in bold are PA athletes.

For more information and complete results visit:
http://www.usatf.org/events/2008/WorldMastersAthleticsChampionshipsIndoor/

Pacific Association Masters Track & Field Committee Chair Joy Upshaw-Margerum took bronze in both the 60m dash and 60 meter hurdles and established a new American record in the hurdles (old record was 9.13), silver in the long jump, and 5th in the 200.  She was also part of the gold medal 40-49 4x200 relay squad!
