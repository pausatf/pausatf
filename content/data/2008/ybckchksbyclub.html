Coaches, Officials, Volunteers with Background Checks:

California State Law requires us to check background information for our current members only.

Due to this law, if you failed to renew your USATF membership by April 1st, you will not be cleared for 2008 until you (1) renew your USATF membership and (2) get another background check at your own expense.
