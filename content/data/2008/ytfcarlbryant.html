Health Scare for Carl Bryant, Central Valley Roadrunners head coach & CA Invite Meet Director

Saturday, May 3, at the California Invitational Relays, meet director & head Central Valley Roadrunners coach, Carl Bryant, had to be taken to the hospital.  At first it was thought he had a heart attack but his condition has been downgraded in severity.  Carl has been released from the hospital and is anxiously looking forward to working with all the long jumpers at the PAC and Junior Olympic meets.  Thanks for all your prayers for his health and safety.  Carl can be reached at 1713 Leilani Way, Ceres, CA  95307.
