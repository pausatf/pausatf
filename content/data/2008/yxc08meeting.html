YOUTH CROSS COUNTRY MEETING

Saturday, June 21, 2008

At the Pacific Association Track & Field Junior Olympics

 Granada High School - Livermore, California

The meeting will take place on Saturday near the start of the 1500m right after the finish of the last 3000m run (at about 10:30).

 

We’ll set up our 2008 schedule and have the biddings for the September, October and November meets.  The first meet we will do the bidding for will be the Pacific Association/Junior Olympic meets and go back from there.  Anybody having ideas or suggestions to improve the program and increase the number of participants please bring them up.  We will talk about once again renting a big tent with heaters for all of us to use at the Nationals.  The Region 14/JOs will be in our Pacific Association area this year.

If your club is going to host a meet I suggest that you pick a few dates and verify those dates with a park before you bid for a date.

  

Our PA/JO meet will be on Sunday, November 16th.

The Region 14/JO meet (Bantams & Midgets) will be on Sunday, November 23rd.

The Region 14/JO meet (Youth, Intermediate & Young) will be on Sun., November 30th. 

The National JOs will be near Richmond Virginia on Saturday, Dec. 13th.

The Nationals for next year (2009) will be in Reno, Nevada.

 

If you have any questions contact Bob Shor, Youth Cross Country Chairman.

(707) 539-6787 or ASHORSR@AOL.COM

 

See you at Granada High School!

  

June 21, 2008
