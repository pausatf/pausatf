Officials Needed for PA-LDR Grand Prix Events

 

PA/USATF LDR OFFICIALS
(and Track Officials who have expressed interest in LDR):

Now that the local track season has wound down...and you thought you  could plan a little break following the Olympic Games...

August is here, which means the Pacific Association's annual Cross  Country Grand Prix Series starts later this month--while concurrently our  LDR Road Running Grand Prix continues in full swing.

So, as you suspected, I'm here to call upon your generous assistance to  officiate at these exciting races. Referees, Judges, Umpires, Timers, and  occasionally Starters are all needed. (In addition, on-call Jury of  Appeal members for XC are sought.)

Several members of our capable officiating force have already volunteered  to work our late summer and fall road events. Thank you. I have penciled  you in. However, we seek additional assistance! In particular, we welcome  new officials--that is, new folks to LDR officiating and seasoned  veterans who, perhaps, haven't volunteered at an LDR/XC event for a  while. Of course, we provide on-the-job training.

Shown below is the 2008 Cross Country Grand Prix schedule, plus the  remaining events on this year's Road Running Grand Prix schedule.  Please...please...let me know as soon as possible if you can contribute  at any of these events.

 

Regards,
