Athletes,
 
As a reminder, please bring your passports to the Olympic Track & Field Team Trials.
 
The top six finishers in each event (top 8 in the 100m and 400m) will be asked to complete Olympic Games processing. Athletes who are to be nominated to the 2008 Olympic Team must declare their intent to represent the U.S. in Beijing no later than the morning following the final of their event. Athletes whose finals are on July 5 and 6 must complete the team processing procedures on the day of their final event. Processing of athletes will include photographs, travel planning, uniform sizing, and team registration. One representative selected by the athlete may be present at the Olympic Team processing.
 
Team processing will be located in Playwrights Hall located on the lobby level of the Eugene Hilton Conference Center (66 E. Sixth Avenue). Athletes who qualify to complete team processing should arrive promptly at the dates and times found at the following link.

http://www.usatf.org/events/2008/OlympicTrials-TF/athleteInfo/teamSignup.asp.

All the best.
 
See you in Eugene,
