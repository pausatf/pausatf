Pacific Association Awards Olympic Training Grants

The Pacific Association put in motion a Pacific Association Olympic Training Grant.  The initial grant was for $7,500 which ensured  five $1,000 and five $500 grants to help defer some of the training expense of our elite athletes.  Since only seven athletes applied, The PA Board unanimously voted to award all seven the sum of $1,000.  The recipients are:
Benjamin Bruce (3,000m sc)
Kevin Davis (3,000m sc)
Kara June (3,000m sc)
Allison Kerr (marathon)
Rachael Longfors (Discus)
Suzy Powell-Roos (Discus)
Stephanie Brown-Trafton (Discus).
We wish them all the best of luck in the coming months.
