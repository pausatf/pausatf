Pacific Association United States of America Track and Field Championship Meets - 2006

Volunteer List

(Revised 4/21/06)

 

 

The following are lists for individual and track team working positions for the upcoming Pacific Association Championship track meets.  Some people have already contacted us for their desired positions while others have been asked to handle certain positions.  We want every team interested in receiving part of the travel fund money disbursement to contact us.   Unattached athletes cannot receive funds from the association but we appreciate help from their family and friends as well. 

 

Our plan is to be able to rotate personnel and have shifts in most positions where possible.    Please look over the lists carefully and decide where you and/or your team wish to work.  Let us know via email and it will be added to the roster for that particular track meet.  If your name is on our list and it should not be there, let us know; it will be removed.  If your name was inadvertently left off and it should be on our list; let us know; it will be added.  If you can think of additional positions needed that are not listed, please let us know that as well.  We want all conceivable areas covered. Remember, our track meets can be successful only if we work together to make them a success.

 

The five track meets hosted by the Youth Committee this year are:

 

·        PAC Championships – Saturday and Sunday, June 10-11, 2006

·        JO Championships – Friday, Saturday and Sunday, June 23-25 2006

·        Subbantam Championships – Saturday, July 1, 2006

·        Region 14 JO Championships – Friday, Saturday and Sunday, July 7-9, 2006

·        Region 14 Multi Event Championships – Saturday and Sunday, July 15-16, 2006

(There is no sign up sheet for the multi meet.  That will be handled on the day of the meet.)

 

On Friday afternoon June 23, at Granada High School, athletes born in 1988-1991 (1987 if born after July 30) will contest the Long Jump.  The same applies for Friday afternoon July 7, the Long Jump and Hammerthrow will be contested at Folsom High School.  The Hammerthrow is still not included as an official event for the JO Nationals but our association has added it as an exhibition event.  Hammerthrow experts are welcome to contact us regarding the officiating of this event.  Your expertise is greatly needed and appreciated.  If you can help us out with either of our Friday afternoon events, please contact Mike Hammerquist at demiurgoi@yahoo.com.  (510) 848-8583)
