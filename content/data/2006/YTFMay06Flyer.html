Umoja Track Club Sectional 2006

 

The packet pick-up table will be located at the entrance to the track, and packets available starting at 7:30am. Please check in immediately after the first call for each event. Runners check in with the Clerk of the Course. Field events will be checked in at the event location. The races and field events will begin right after the final call. The field events will start at 8:30am, and the first running event will start at 9:00 am. All running events will begin with the youngest age division with the girls going first in the order below unless otherwise specified. Field events will run in age division order as specified. Each athlete will have three jumps or throws, no finals, and must supply own implement for shot put and mini javelin. Sub-Bantam, Bantam and Midget divisions may participate in three events. Youth and Open may participate in four events.

 

Ribbons will be awarded for 1st - 6th place. Entry fees are $6.00 per athlete, non-refundable. Please pre-register and send your entry fees no later than April 28th to Umoja Track Club, 951 Rock Canyon Circle, San Jose, CA 95127. There will be NO DAY OF THE MEET ENTRIES.
