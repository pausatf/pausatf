Zippy Time Bonuses

In addition to the standard PA prize money, the Zippy 5K on Sunday, April 23, is offering bonuses to a winning PA male who breaks 14:30 and winning PA female who breaks the course record of 16:52. The bonus is calculated as $20 per second (male)/$25 per second (female) under these time standards, up to $500. (The bonus increases $5 every time the standard is broken--and the faster time becomes the new standard.)

Go to www.zippy5krun.org or contact Tyler Abbott for more information, and we look forward to seeing you at Zippy! /html
