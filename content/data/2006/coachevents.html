USATF Coaches Education Upcoming Schools and Summits
updated 13 October, 2006

USATF Coaches Education is dedicated to increasing the knowledge and effectiveness of coaches of all levels throughout the United States.  Over 23,000 coaches have taken advantage of these all encompassing learning opportunities. 
For more information and updates, also refer to: http://www.usatf.org/groups/Coaches/education/, or contact pacific association coaches chair Dave Shrock at: dshrock@pacbell.net

Upcoming Clinics: 

Thursday December 7, 2006.  6:00-8:00 PM. Asics World Distance Summit  
Features several of the top coaches and personalities from the world of distance running who will 
answer questions in a round robin format.
Where:  Holiday Inn Fisherman's Wharf
550 North Point St.
San Francisco, CA
800-942-7348
Presenters include:
Steve Scott:  US Mile Record Holder at 3:47.69 and has broken 4 minutes in the mile 136 times.  
One of the greatest milers in American History. Current coach at Cal State University San Marcos.
Dr Armando Siqueiros.  Former 29:12 10k runner and past winner of the Wharf to Wharf race,  
Currently coaches at Mission Prep High School in San Luis Obispo and is coach of defending 
Footlocker National Champion Jordan Hasay.
Jim Spivey:  Former Women¹s head coach at Vanderbuilt University.  Former sub 3:50 miler and 
1987 World Championships bronze medalist.  Also holds the US record for 2,000m.
Dr Joe Vigil.  Legendary distance running personality, coach and clinician. Served as 1988 US 
Olympic Coach, former coach of multiple national champions Adams State College as well as serving 
on multiple US World Cross Country teams as Head Coach.  Vigil gained additional accolades as
coach of Deena Kastor, 2004 Olympic Marathon Bronze medalist.
Mike Fanelli:  Mike serves as emcee of the event.  Outstanding moderator who possesses a wealth 
of information on the history of competitive distance running as well as the physiology of training.  
Mike is entertaining and knowledgeable.

Cost: Absolutely FREE.  This is a Christmas present from Asics, the Pacific Association, the Cal Coaches 
Alliance, American Track and Field, Cliff Bar, Pacific Coast Sportswear and Running Warehouse 
to the coaches and fans of distance running in California.  This is an opportunity NOT to be missed.
