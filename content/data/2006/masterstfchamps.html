USA Masters Outdoor Track & Field Championships

Entry Deadline extended to July 14!

Entry and meet information on this summer's USA Masters Outdoor Track & Field Championships to be held August 3-6 in Charlotte, NC is now available online at www.usatf.org/events .

Nearly 1,000 of the nation's finest athletes over the age of 30 are expected to compete in Charlotte for national age division bragging-rights.
