Mark Springer Youth Travel Grant Program Information

In partnership with the USA Track & Field Foundation, USATF announces the 2006 Mark Springer Youth Travel Grant program to aid in bringing athletes to USATF's four National Youth and Junior championship events this year:

6/22-25
USA
Junior Outdoor T&F Championships
Indianapolis, IN

6/27 - 7/2
USA
Youth Outdoor T&F Championships
Greensboro, NC

7/25-30
USATF

National Junior Olympic T&F Championships
Baltimore, MD

12/9
USATF
National Junior Olympic XC Championships
Spokane, WA
For additional information on the travel grant program visit
https://www.usatf.org/clubs/benefits/travelGrants/.
The deadline to submit grant applications is May 1, 2006. Clubs must have their club membership renewed prior to April 21, 2006 be eligible to receive a grant.
