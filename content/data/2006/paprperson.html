Pacific Association seeking PR person - paid position


At the January 2006 Board of Athletics meeting, Pacific Association/USATF approved a motion to hire a person who would handle Youth Committee public relations for the next six months. The work would involve writing articles and press releases highlighting youth meets, teams and individuals, and submitting this material to as many media outlets as possible.

For more information contact Pacific Association Executive Director John Mansoor by email, pausatf@aol.com, or by phone: 916/983-4715. Email a resume to pausatf@aol.com or mail it to: PA/USATF, 120 Ponderosa Ct., Folsom, CA 95630
