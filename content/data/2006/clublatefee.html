Late Fee Implemented For Club Membership Renewals Received After May 1

Clubs are reminded that starting this year a $100 late fee will be charged if your club's membership is not renewed prior to May 1.

This late fee only applies to renewing competitive clubs. Organizational memberships (event organizers, timing companies, corporations, etc.) and clubs who do not have athletes representing the club in sanctioned competitions are exempt from this late fee but still encouraged to renew their membership now. The late fee is in addition to the annual club membership fee.

If you have not renewed your club membership please visit www.usatf.org/clubs/application/download/ and download a club application today.
