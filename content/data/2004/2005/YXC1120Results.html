TEAM SCORING BY AGE GROUPS.
  
The first three teams in each age group advance to the Region 14 
Championships on Sunday, November 27th in Fresno.  Scoring is 
figured only for team members, not for unattached athletes or 
incomplete teams.  There must be 5 to 8 runners to be a team.  
The score is figured by adding up the places of the first five 
runners of each team.  The lowest scoring team is the winner.  
If there is a tie then the fastest runner of the fifth place 
finisher of the tied teams will be the winner.
