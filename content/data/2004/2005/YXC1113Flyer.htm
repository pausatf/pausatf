Awards:  Medals to top 10 finishers in each race (top 5 for Open) and ribbons to all other finishers, presented after each race

Race Entry Fee:  $6.00/athlete.  Make checks payable to Pleasanton Heat.  Please be prepared to pay a $6 parking fee to Shadow Cliffs Regional Park.

Instructions:  Please have all athletes complete the ATHLETE’S RELEASE and have the releases available at registration.  Teams must wear matching tops. The meet is to be governed by USATF Rule 240.  Sub-Bantams may run in the Bantam races.  Other competitors may only compete in their sex/age group.  An athlete’s year of birth shall determine the division in which they compete.  Submission of falsified or altered documents will result in disqualification of the athlete, and if it involves two or more athletes from a club, the entire team will be disqualified.  Protests concerning the status or eligibility of an entrant must be made to the meet coordinator and director.

MAP TO SHADOW CLIFFS REGIONAL PARK

 

Shadow Cliffs is located at 2500 Stanley Boulevard in Pleasanton, CA.

 

From Highway 580:

From I-580 in Pleasanton, take the Airway Boulevard exit and turn south, towards Pleasanton. Turn right on Kitty Hawk Road, which becomes Isabel Avenue. Turn right on Stanley Boulevard and proceed to the park entrance about 2.2 miles on the left. 

 

Please do not take El Charro Blvd between 580 and Stanley as there is a gate across the road preventing access to Stanley Blvd.

 

From Highway 680:

Exit from 680 on Sunol Blvd.  Take Sunol Blvd. to the right toward Pleasanton.

After about a mile, Sunol Blvd. becomes First Street. After another mile First Street becomes Stanley Blvd.  The park is on your right about 1 mile after passing through the stoplight at Valley Ave.
