Pacific USATF 1-Mile Grand Prix Race Walk

8/15/2004

Sponsored by Sierra Racewalkers and Pacific Racewalkers Organization

Sanctioned by Pacific USATF

 

 

Ø      Time: 10AM – Men’s race, 10:45AM - Women’s Race

 

Ø      Location: Bella Vista High School Track, 8301 Madison, Fair Oaks, CA

 

Ø      Entry Fee: $10 pre-entry postmarked by 8/7/2004.   $15 after 8/7 and on race day.  Do not mail entries after 8/7.

 

Ø      Entry:  Race is open, with no standards.  Pacific USATF membership is required for grand prix scoring – for more information or to purchase a membership online, go to www.usatf.org

 

Ø      Contact: Ed Parrot at edndana@pacbell.net

 

Ø      Web Site: http://www.pausatf.org

 

Ø      Directions:

If coming from the north or west, it is recommended that you take highway 50, not Interstate 80 once you get into Sacramento.  If coming from highway 50, either direction, take the Sunrise Ave exit heading NORTH on Sunrise.  You will go through a number of lights, cross the American River, and then go through several more lights.  Take a right when you get to Madison Ave.  If coming from the east on I-80, take the Sunrise Ave exit heading SOUTH on Sunrise.  You will go through many lights, and then take a left on Madison Ave.  The school is several miles down Madison on the left.  Park in the school parking lot.
