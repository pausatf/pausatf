                        Today's youth athletes are tomorrow's champions! As the #1 high school and junior high school participatory sport, Track & Field, Cross Country, and Race walking offer a variety of competitive and fitness opportunities.

                         

                        The Pacific Association is proud to announce that it will be hosting the 44th Annual USATF National Junior Olympic Track & Field Championships. USATF's largest youth program where nearly 70,000 youth athletes compete each year in the Track & Field and Cross Country championships.

                         

                        The event will take place July 27 - August 1, 2010 at Hughes Stadium, Sacramento City College - Sacramento, California. Where America's next generation of track and field stars will take center stage.

                         

                        Join USATF today and make your Olympic dreams come true!
