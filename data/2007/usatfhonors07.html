PACIFIC ASSOCIATION ATHLETES, OFFICIALS RECOGNIZED AT USATF ANNUAL MEETING

HONOLULU - At the Annual Awards Breakfast on December 1 at the 2007 USA Track & Field Annual Meeting in Honolulu, numerous Pacific Association/USATF awards winners were announced for their achievements on and off the track. Winners are listed below:

--Masters Long Distance Running --

Masters Age Division Runners of the Year: Age 80-84: Joy Johnson (San Jose, Calif.)

--Masters Track & Field Committee--

Masters Men Athletes of the Year 60+: Ed Burke (Los Gatos, Calif.)

Masters Women Athletes of the Year: Ages 30-59: Joy Upshaw-Margerum
(Los Altos Hills, Calif.)

Masters Race Walkers of the Year

Age 50-54: Mark Green (Reno, Nev.)
Age 70-74: Shirley Dockstader (Mill Valley, Calif.), Jack Bray
(Greenbrae, Calif.)
Age: 85-89: John Levinsohn (Greenbrae, Calif.)

--Mountain & Ultrarunning Awards--

Mountain Runners of the Year: Christine Lundy (Sausalito, Calif.)

Masters Ultrarunners of the Year: Beverly Anderson-Abbs (Red Bluff,
Calif.)

--USATF Associations Awards--

Bronze Awards: Pacific

--Officials Committee Awards--

Officials Hall of Fame Inductees: Horace Crow (deceased)

Horace Crow Award: George Kleeman (Martinez, Calif.)

--Men's Long Distance Running Awards--

Scott Hamilton Memorial Award (Association Level Contributor): Tyler
Abbott (San Francisco, Calif.)

--U.S. Track Meet Directors Association Awards--

Howard Schmertz Lifetime Achievement Award: Tom Moore (Modesto
Relays, deceased)

--Men's Track & Field Awards--

Roy Griakk Distinguished Service Award: Bob Podkaminer (Santa Rosa,
Calif.)

For more information on the 2007 USATF Annual Meeting, visit:
