Club News and Notes: March 23, 2007


Club Marathon Relay Championships to offer $30,000

The 2007 USATF National Club Marathon Relay Championships will be held May 20 in Denver, Colorado. The championships are being hosted by the Colfax Colorado Marathon for the second year. $30,000 ($15,000 men, $15,000 women) in prize money will be distributed to the top teams. Last year Boulder Express "BX" (Colorado) and adidas Raleigh TC (North Carolina) claimed the national titles for men and women, respectively.
