Inaugural Sectional Youth Meet

Saturday, May 12, 2007

College of San Mateo

1700 W. Hillsdale Blvd.

San Mateo, CA 94402

 

 

 

Starting Time – The field events and first running event both start at 9:00 a.m.

 

Packet Pick-up – begins at 8:00 a.m.

 

Coaches Meeting – begins at 8:30 a.m. to go over meet rules and day of meet scratches. 

 

Track Equipment – Spikes must be compression (Xmas tree) spikes, no longer than 1/4.”  Pin spikes will not be allowed, as a condition of our use of the facility.  Spikes will be checked when athletes check-in for events – if replacement spikes are needed, they will be available for purchase.

 

Tents/Canopies – Space is limited and tents/canopies will only be allowed at the top of the bleachers. 

 

Entries

Fees - $6.00 per athlete

 

Number of Events – Sub-bantams, Bantams, and Midgets may participate in three events.  Youth and Open may participate in four events. 

 

Entries Deadline – Entries must be postmarked or e-mailed by May 7th.

 

Payment Information – All payments must be:

·       Club Check, Certified Check, or Money Order only

·       Made payable to:                             Community Development Institute

·       Postmarked by May 7th to:  East Palo Alto Greyhounds 2007 Track Meet,

2200 Oakwood Drive, East Palo Alto, CA 94303

Order of Events

Starting Time – The field events and first running event both start at 9:00 a.m. 

 

All races will begin with the youngest age group, first girls, and then boys.  Field events have three jumps or throws; no finals. 

 

Awards – Ribbons will be awarded to all participants in each heat.  Medals will be awarded for overall winners in each age group.

 

 

Track Events are on a rolling schedule, beginning at 9:00 a.m.

1500m Racewalk (Bantam, Midget)

110m Hurdles (Open Boys)

Driving Directions:

Merge onto CA-92 W toward SAN MATEO BR. (toll) (15.9 mi.)

Exit at WEST HILLSDALE BLVD exit (0.2 mi), turning RIGHT at the traffic light at the end of the exit ramp onto WEST HILLSDALE BLVD.  Proceed up hill through second set of traffic lights. Parking: Parking for the meet will be available in lots 14, 18, 21, and 22.  Park in marked stalls only in student parking lots/areas only (parking is free on Saturdays in these areas).  Cars parked in staff lots or in unmarked spaces may be ticketed and/or towed.
