Rio Vista River Vault - May 12, 2007


The Rio Vista River Vault will be held on Front St. in Rio Vista on May 12, starting at 2 pm.  There will be a full women's and men's field including the likes of American Record Holder Jeff Hartwig, Olympic Silver Medalist Toby Stevenson, World Champion Brad Walker, Korean Record Holder Yoo Kim, National Champion Russ Buller, Olympic Silver Medalist Nick Hysong, Worlds Silver Medalist Tye Harvey, Gold Medalist Stacy Dragela, Olympian Jillian Schwartz, Becky Holliday, Tracy O'Hara, Mary Sauer, Kate Soma, and a hand full more.

There will be food and drinks available, music, auction, give-aways, autograph signings, and world class pole vaulting (no-heighters get tossed into the river).  Come join the fun! For more information to to www.tigerbarsports.com
