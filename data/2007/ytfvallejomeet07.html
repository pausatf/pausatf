Youth All Comer Track & Field Meet

Vallejo High School,    840 Nebraska Street

Saturday,   July 14, 2007

 

Field events and first running event both start at 9:00 a.m.  All races will begin with the youngest age group and the girls will go first.  Field events have four jumps or throws; no finals.  Events will be run in the order below unless otherwise specified.  Sub-bantam, Bantam, and Midget divisions may participate in three events.  Youth, Intermediate and Young Women/Men may participate in four events.  Concession stand available.  $5.00 per athlete entry fee.

 

Meet Directors: Joanne Slaton-Camargo at (510) 430-8223 or Coach Mike Wilson at (707) 552-8382
