A casting agent we have worked with in the past is looking for athletes
for a Canon commercial which will shoot in Japan in late January.

They are looking for one long jumper (male or female), 1 high jumper
(male or female) and 2-5 MALE 100m sprinters.

To be considered for the commercial, the athlete needs to send in video
footage from the last six months, and they need to NOT be a contender to
make the Olympic Team. They need somebody who will sign a form saying
they will NOT be in competing in Beijing at the Olympics. Also the
athlete can't be required to wear sponsored or club apparel during the
shoot.

No NCAA-eligible athletes (this includes High School students who plan
to compete in college).

Below is all the information, include pay rates, requirements, etc. If
you have somebody you think might be a good fit, please send an e-mail
to Jill.Geer@usatf.org and she will put you in touch with the agent.

Details

* Any race/ethnicity
* 18 to 30 years olds (or otherwise realistic age to be
competing in Olympics in the particular field)
* Non-Union
* Must have valid passport
* Must be in great shape
* Anyone considered a serious contender for Olympic team in any
country is ineligible (due to the possibility the spots may be broadcast
during the Olympic games)
* Anyone required to wear sportwear sponsor's logo on uniform
is ineligible

SHOOT Dates IN JAPAN - January 21-28th, Includes fitting, travel over
and back, rest days, shoot days, weather day

RATE/Fees:
Travel/Prep/Layover $500/day x 5.5 days = est. $2,750
Shoot (10 hrs/day) $1,000/ day x 2 days = $2,000
Usage $3,000/year
Per Diems $75/day x 6 days = est. $450
=> $7,750 fees + $450 per diem

Air Travel (Premier Economy) and Accommodation in Japan will be arranged
by production.

Spot: 60 sec. and 30 sec. lifts

Usage: TV Broadcast (incl. Satellite and Cable), Internet, Mobile, POP,
and Events such as trade shows
Duration: One Year
Territory: China
Exclusivity: Camera related ads in China
