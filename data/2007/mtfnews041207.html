Mt Sac Relays 2007

PA masters Athletes Aaron Thigpen & Marty Krulee Winners!!

Aaron 100m M40 American Record!  10.60 (2nd in an "Open" heat)

Aaron also wins 200m finals in 22.07.

Marty Krulee wins 50+ 100m finals in 11.54

Pacific Association's Aaron Thigpen Sets Masters 100m Record

 

A U.S. masters 100-meter record was set by Aaron Thigpen (Brentwood, CA) in the M40 division at the 49th annual Mt.  San Antonio College Relays in Walnut, California, April 13-15.  Thigpen’s time of 10.60 seconds bettered his previous AR of 10.73 set last year in Charlotte, N.C. Thigpen, also, won the masters 200m dash in  22.07. In addition, PA/USATF's Marty Krulee of Rocklin, CA won the M50 division 100m dash in 11.54 seconds.
