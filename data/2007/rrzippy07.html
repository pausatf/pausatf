Zippy Time Bonuses

Zippy 5K - April 29, 2007 - Golden Gate Park


As it has the last couple of years, the Zippy 5K on Sunday, April 29 is offering bonuses to the Pacific Association winner who bests certain times at Zippy.

For women, the time to beat is the course record of 16:22, and the bonus is $30/second with a maximum of $500 (every time the record is broken, Zippy adds $5/second to the bonus).

For men, the time to beat is the standard of 14:30, and the bonus is $20/second, with a maximum of $500
