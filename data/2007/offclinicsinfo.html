OFFICIALS CLINICS

The officials' clinics for 2007 are now complete since we have started the outdoor season.

However, if you interested in becoming a certified official, send an email to Jim Hume, our certification chair, at jimhume.certchair@sbcglobal.net.

To receive notice in the fall of the officiating clinics to be held in 2008, please email George Kleeman at george_kleeman@comcast.net.  The schedule will be available in early November.  Watch this space.

Start officiating now!

You need not wait till next season to start officiating. Go to your local high school, Junior College or college and talk to the coach and volunteer to help. 

Or look at the officials' meet schedule on this website--behind “Calendar” in the officials section-- for other opportunities. 

Just contact the person listed to say you’re coming and what events you would like to work.  We can always use your help.

Topics and help for 2008

To suggest topics that we should teach at clinics next year, or to volunteer to help put on clinics, contact George Kleeman at the e-mail above.
