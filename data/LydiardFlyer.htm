Arthur Lydiard Clinic in San Francisco

 

Don’t miss the final opportunity to meet this Living Legend, the man who started it all!

 

Arthur Lydiard revolutionized modern endurance training and invented “JOGGING”.  He coached and advised/influenced 18 Olympic medalists that include Peter Snell of New Zealand (3 gold medals) and Lasse Viren of Finland (4 gold medals). 

Evening with Arthur Lydiard
 

 

 


Lydiard is on his Final USA Lecture Tour and is making a stop in San Francisco on Sunday, December 19, 2004.  There will be film footage of “Arthur’s Boys” and Power-Point presentation on the Lydiard training method and hill training video, followed by Q&A by Lydiard himself.
