Track & Field Chair Position Open

There is a vacancy in the position of Men's Track and Field Chair and in Women's Track and Field Chair in The Pacific Association USATF.  Any interested parties please contact Jerry Colman, President PAUSATF, at sactc@aol.com to nominate yourself or someone else.  Voting will commence February 15

QUALIFICATIONS

                   1.Knowledgeable in the sport. 
                   2.Arrange for and oversee The Pacific Association Open T&F Championships.
                   3.Cumulate and score The PA T/F Grand Prix.
