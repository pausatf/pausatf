OFFICIALS TRAINING ASSOCIATES SOUGHT

Calling for certified officials to serve as Training Associates!

The duties: To assist George Kleeman, Training Chair, with administrative and clerical tasks relating to the clinics. Typical jobs might include registering students at a clinic...dealing with accounting after a clinic...handling paperwork and communications.

Are we looking for just one person? No: there is work enough for several volunteers.

The pay? None, except the thanks of a grateful officiate.

The rewards? Better understanding of how our organization works; opportunities to meet and interact with more of your fellow officials.

The hours, days, work load? Negotiable. Most of the work is done in December and January-and doesn't necessarily involve teaching.

Interested? Then contact Mr. Kleeman directly at george_kleeman@comcast.net.
