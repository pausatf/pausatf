SANTA ROSA EXPRESS

YOUTH CROSS COUNTRY MEET

SUNDAY, OCTOBER 22, 2006

Spring Lake Park in Santa Rosa, California

Entry Fee - $6 Per Athlete           Cash or Check (Payable to ‘SRX’)

 

The course is a grass, gravel, dirt and asphalt with rolling hills.  No spikes allowed.

 

        Sub-Bantam Girls/Sub-Bantam Boys         Born 1998 or later               2000 meters

        Bantam Girls/Bantam Boys                        Born 1996 or 1997              3000 meters

        Midget Girls/ Midget Boys                         Born 1994 or 1995             3000 meters

       Youth Girls/Youth Boys                              Born 1992 or 1993             4000 meters

       Intermediate Girls/Intermediate Boys          Born 1990 or 1991             5000 meters

       Young Women/Young Men                         Born 1988 or 1989             5000 meters

 

The races will be run in the order above.  Some races may be combined (depending on the number of runners) but  the results and awards will be determined by sex and age group. 

 

TIME SCHEDULE

Turn in Entry.....8:00 to 9:00

Course Walk.....9:00 to 10:00

First Race (Sub-Bantams).....10:00

The next race starts shortly after the last runner in the previous race crosses the finish line

 

Registration and the Start/Finish Area is on the West Side of the Park so you will have to Walk Around the Lake

Directions to Spring Lake Park

 

FROM US 101: In Santa Rosa head east on Highway 12 to the end of the freeway.  Continue ahead on Hoen Avenue.  Go about 2 miles (NEW DIRECTIONS) and turn left on Newanga Avenue.  Newanga runs into the park.  When you go in the gate turn right and park up above.

 

 

FROM I-80: Just west of Fairfield, get onto Highway 12 going west (toward Napa).  Stay on Highway 12, passing near Napa, then through Sonoma.  About 15 miles west of Sonoma, turn left on (NEW DIRECTIONS) Los Alamos Road, then right on Melita which will become Montgomery Drive to the left.  Go about one mile on Montgomery and turn left on Channel Drive.  Around the first turn, turn right on Violetti Road.  Enter the park on your right and go to the bottom of the hill.

 

FROM I-5 HEADING SOUTH: Go west on Highway 20 near Williams.  Turn left onto Highway 53, which will soon become Highway 29.  When you get to the bottom of the unending hill, just before you get to Calistoga, turn right onto Tubbs Lane.  When it ends, turn left on Foothill for a very short distance and turn right at the stop sign onto Petrified Forest Road (which will later become Calistoga Road).  Be sure to follow the sign to Highway 12.  About 1 mile after you get to the bottom of the winding hill, turn right onto Highway 12.  Go about 1½ miles, turn left onto   Mission Blvd., (NEW DIRECTIONS) then left onto Montgomery Drive and go about two miles.  Turn right on Channel Drive.  Around the first turn, turn right on Violetti Road.  Enter the park on your right and go to the bottom of the hill.

 

We have requested free parking from the Sonoma County Parks and Recreation.  As of this date it has not been authorized.  When and if it is, make as many copies of the permits as you need.
