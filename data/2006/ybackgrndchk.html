IMPORTANT UPDATE ON PA/USATF BACKGROUND CHECK PROGAM

Due to yet another delay by the Department of Justice in processing our application I regret to inform you that we will NOT be able to do the live scan/fingerprinting on Wednesday, Nov. 14 as planned in Walnut Creek.

 

Once we do receive the material from the Department of Justice in the next couple of weeks I will send out locations near you where you can have the live scan/fingerprinting performed.

 

Some of these locations will not allow us to establish a master billing account. This will require you to pay for the scan, and then submit the receipt to my office for reimbursement.

 

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause you.

 

Please note that in order to be scanned you will need to be a 2007 USATF member first. You can do this online at PAUSTF.org

 

Thank you for your support of this new and valuable program.
