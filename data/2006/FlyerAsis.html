Asics World Distance Summit

Mark your calendar for Thursday, Dec 7, 6-8 PM


What:Features several of the top coaches and personalities from the world 
of distance running who will answer questions in a round robin format.

Presenters include:

Steve Scott:  US Mile Record Holder at 3:47.69 and has broken 4 minutes in
the mile 136 times.  One of the greatest milers in American History.
Current coach at Cal State University San Marcos.

Dr Armando Siqueiros.  Former 29:12 10k runner and past winner of the 
Wharf to Wharf race,  Currently coaches at Mission Prep High School in San Luis
Obispo and is coach of defending Footlocker National Champion Jordan Hasay.

Jim Spivey:  Former Women¹s head coach at Vanderbuilt University.  Former
sub 3:50 miler and 1987 World Championships bronze medalist.  Also holds 
the US record for 2,000m.

Dr Joe Vigil.  Legendary distance running personality, coach and clinician.
Served as 1988 US Olympic Coach, former coach of multiple national team
championship Adams State College as well as serving on multiple US World
Cross Country teams as Head Coach.  Vigil gained additional accolades as
coach of Deena Kastor, 2004 Olympic Marathon Bronze medal.

Mike Fanelli:  Mike serves as emcee of the event.  Outstanding moderator 
who possesses a wealth of information on the history of competitive distance
running as well as the physiology of training.  Mike is entertaining and
knowledgeable.

Where:  Holiday Inn Fisherman's Wharf
