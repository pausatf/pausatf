Press Release, 5/24

 

Facilities: On the campus of College of San Mateo. Nine (9) lane Mondo surface throughout, including steeplechase, javelin approaches and all runways. Required spike is ¼ inch Christmas tree (compression). The throwing rings are brushed concrete.

Eligibility: All competitors must be a registered USATF Athlete. Only collegiate athletes who compete for their institutions and are registered by their coaches are not required to be USATF member. USATF membership cards are available for $20.00 by contacting the Pacific Association Office at (916) 983-4715, or you may download an application on-line from .pausatf.org. Memberships will also be on sale the day of the meet..

Entry Fees:    ON-LINE PREREGISTRATION: $15.00 per event if entry is received by 7:00 p.m. Thursday, May 25, 2006, payable by credit card. No telephone, mail, or fax entries.

LATE ENTRY - MEET DAY REGISTRATION: $30.00 per event. CASH ONLY. The late entry deadline is 9:00 a.m. for lane events and 9: 30 a.m. for field events. NO EXCEPTIONS.

Entry Form:   “DirectAthletics (http://www.directathletics.com/) Questions regarding entries? Contact Al Hernandez at (510) 651-3162 or email: runalrun@yahoo.com


Event Registered track contestants check-in with the running clerk at least 30 minutes

Check-In: prior to the event start time. All athletes must check in at registration table before competition. Field events should check in 45 minutes prior to event start at event site.

Heats/Flights: All running events will be run as finals only with seeding based on submitted marks.

Organization: Field event competitors will have three initial attempts with the top (9) nine competitors    qualifying for the finals. Weights and measures will be from  9:00 a.m. to  12:30 p.m.


Further Info: Contact Al Hernandez, (510) 651-3162, or via e-mail: runalrun@yahoo.com

PAUSATF Grand Prix:  Earn double points towards prize monies being awarded by the Pacific Association Open T&F Committee. To register, you must be a current PA-USATF member. The Pacific Association Grand Prix is a series, culminating with these Association Championships. $15,000.00 will be awarded to the Association’s top ten men and top ten women utilizing the 2001 IAAF Scoring Table. Contact Women’s T & F Chair Fred Baer at (651) 345-4114, or email: Frdbaer@aol.com for further information and to register.
