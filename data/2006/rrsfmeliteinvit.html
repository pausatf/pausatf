About The San Francisco Marathon(tm)
Since its first running in 1977, The San Francisco Marathon(tm) has
enjoyed success as the premier summertime marathon in the U.S. San
Francisco's blend of natural beauty, big city charm, and perfect
mid-summer running temperatures, make marathon running in San Francisco a
truly magical experience. The San Francisco Marathon's unique Cause to
Run program allows race participants to conveniently contribute to any of
the event's beneficiaries. Since Cause to Run was started by the marathon
in 2003, the program has raised over $250,000 for over 50 charity
organizations. Visit www.runsfm.com for more information.
