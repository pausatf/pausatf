Livescan Update:

Individuals who have been removed for Background Clearance List

The following is a listing of the individuals for which a "No Longer Interested Letter" was forwarded to the Department of Justice, to remove each individual from the PAUSATF Background Clearance Listing.  Most were for a failure to renew their membership prior to April 15, 2008.

For each of these individuals: they must now pay for and resubmit a livescan application and a Coach/Volunteer packet...along with a 2008 USATF Membership Number... to be reinstated in the Background Cleared Listing for the PAUSATF.

If you feel that there is an error in this listing, and can provide documentary support to show an error on our part, please submit your information to George H. Paddeck at ghpadd@yahoo.com.
