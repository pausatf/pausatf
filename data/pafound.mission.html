Pacific Association USATF Foundation Mission



The mission of the Pacific Association USATF Foundation is:

To further the support and development of the sports of track & field,
long distance running, and race walking through annual contributions to:
  • Athletes
  • Officials
  • Coaches
  • Clubs
  • Committees
  • Programs whose purpose is to support and develop the above activities.
