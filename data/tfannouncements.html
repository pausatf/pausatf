Trials SuperClinic website:
http://www.usatf.org/groups/Coaches/education/specialPrograms/2008/SuperClinic
Tuesday, 1 July, 2008 U.S. Olympic Team Trials Super Clinic, Hosted by the High Performance Division and Coaching Education of U.S. A. Track and Field:  South Eugene H.S. (8 blocks from Hayward Field) More than 45 of the nation's best coaches and sports scientists will share their knowledge on what enabled the nation's best athletes to qualify for the U.S. Olympic Team Trials - Track & Field and the Beijing Olympic Games.
Registration begins at 7:30 a.m., with introductory and five event-specific sessions from 8:30 a.m. - 5:30 p.m. Registration is free for USATF members.  Super Clinic t-shirts and session notes will be available for the first 500 attendees who pre-register online via the USATF website.  On-site registration will be available for $30.00 (USATF membership fee), which will also include session notes. Contact Clinic Coordinator Dave Shrock at dshrock@pacbell.net, or 209.602-4023.

Aggies-PCS Sports Olympic Trials-Villard St. Pub Gatherings website:
http://www.pcsportswear.com/2008_tracktown_trials.html
Hosted by ‘Peanut’ Harms, this Trials tradition continues at the Villard Street Pub near Hayward Field.  All welcome. Daily activities planned with bands, clinics, and wide-screen TV’s to provide live Trials coverage.
