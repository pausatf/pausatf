Yahoo eGroup Information

last updated 12/2004
The PA/USATF LDR Committee maintains an eGroup on Yahoo.  
Yahoo provides the service free, and anyone can join the eGroup and send messages 
to the group. The messages are monitored by LDR Committee members, so advertising 
or messages felt to be inappropriate are not released.  

To add yourself to the group, send an email to :

PAUSATF-LDR-subscribe@yahoogroups.com.

You will be added to the group with the address that you sent the note from.  

You can also access advanced options by registering with Yahoo.  This allows you 
to see a list of the members and to choose other email options for your profile. 
For example, you can choose not to receive any email, but to just visit the website 
to browse it. Again, it is not necessary to register to be part of the eGroup.

To register, or to visit the website:

http://groups.yahoo.com

To send a note to the eGroup address it to:

PAUSATF-LDR@yahoogroups.com

To unsubscribe from the eGroup, send a note to:

PAUSATF-LDR-unsubscribe@yahoogroups.com
