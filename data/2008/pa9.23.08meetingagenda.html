PA Board of Athletics Meeting Notice
September 23, 2008

This is a special notice for the Pacific Association Board of Athletics to be held on Tuesday, September 23rd, at the Foster City Recreation Center at 6:30 pm. Foster City Recreation Center is located at the corner of Hillsdale and Shell Blvd.

The special start time of 6:30 p.m. is to allow you an opportunity to meet the new CEO of USA Track & Field, Doug Logan.

Our regular meeting will follow Doug at 7:30 pm
