Los Gatos Summer All-Comers Meets
Every Thursday evening 19 June-August 7, 2008

5:00 PM: PV, HJ; SP then Discus

5:30 PM: LJ then TJ

5:45 PM: 1 Mile RaceWalk

6:00 PM: 4 x 100 Relay, 800, HH, 100, 400, Mile (1500 last meet), 60, 200, 2mile (5K last meet), IH, 4 x 400 Relay

$5.00 Entry Fee unlimited events; $2.00 Spectators-all funds support the LGHS Track and Field program

Further information, contact Rick Milam at milam@nbnsports.com
