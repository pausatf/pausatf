2008 Junior Olympic Track Meet Program

Pacific Association USA Track and Field

Youth Committee

Advertising Price List


It’s time to start on our annual Pacific Association Junior Olympic track and field meet 
program scheduled for June 20-22, 2008. Anthony Camargo is designing our meet program again 
this upcoming year. Please email him regarding any questions pertaining to the art or design 
of your ad. If you have suggestions for our track meet program, please let us know that as 
well. All advertisement information is due to Anthony no later than May 31, 2008. 

The ads will be administered on a first-come first-serve basis. If interested in sponsoring a 
particular event please let us know that information as soon as possible. The first person or 
organization who pays for a particular event will be the sponsor of that event. 

Please make your advertisement print-ready. We will not edit your ad for you. Make all checks 
payable to PA/USATF for exact amount listed below. Mail your payment to: 

      PA/USATF
      c/o Anthony Camargo
      9700 Coral Road
      Oakland, CA 94603

Teams can submit a one-page bio of their team with or without a picture to be included in the 
program. Send bio and picture to Anthony Camargo at ajc_arts@yahoo.com no later than May 31, 2008.

We need your pictures to help complete our program. Outstanding footage is desired for our 
cover page. We want our student athletes to be an integral part of this program. Therefore, 
if you have any action shot pictures of you and/or other athletes from our 2007 and 2008 track 
season that can be used in our program or the cover page, please email them along with the 
proper names and credits for those pictures to Anthony Camargo at ajc_arts@yahoo.com. We also 
want artwork by our student athletes throughout the program. Parents and coaches, will you 
kindly have your athletes contribute hand-drawn pictures that you would like included in the 
program. If possible please, include a short story in their own handwriting, describing their 
picture. Be sure to include their name, age and team affiliation.

The following advertisement prices are available to business and public outside advertisers.

*These four (4) advertisement spaces are reserved for full-page ads only:
      	   *Inside back covers (2): $ 75
    	   *Inside front covers (2): $100

    	    Full page:   $ 50
    	    Half page:   $ 30
    	    Quarter page:  $ 20
    	    Sponsored Events:  $ 50 ½ - ¾ page ad located with that particular event listed 
		                           below by age group and gender

Example: Youth Girls Discus
         Bantam Boys 100 meter dash
         Midget Girls Long Jump
         Young Men 110 meter hurdles

All PA/USATF youth teams can advertise at no cost. However, inside covers are not available 
to youth teams at no cost. Please email your information to Anthony Camargo.
