Subject: Olympian Track and Field Clinic July 12, 2008

Coaches, Parents and Athletes,
 
We are hosting our third annual Olympian Track and Field Clinic, July 12, 2008 at Pittsburgh HS, featuring over a dozen Olympians that will facilitate the clinic, Including Olympic champions, and current World Record Holders. This is an excellent opportunity for you and your youth. The fee for the clinic is only $20.00, and no youth will be turned away because they don't have the fee. We rather have them there getting the knowledge. We will provide lunch and there will be an all comers style meet following the clinic. I realize that many of your athletes will be traveling to other events on that date, however this an opportunity for those youngsters not traveling to get some outstanding coaching and competition.  Additional information and you can sign up on the web at: http://www.eddiehartaiof.org
 
Hope to see you there,
