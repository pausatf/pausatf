Zippy Time Bonuses


As it has the last couple of years, the Zippy 5K on Sunday, April 20 is
offering bonuses to the Pacific Association winner who bests certain times
at Zippy.

For women, the time to beat is the course record of 16:22, and the bonus
is $30/second (every time the record is broken, Zippy adds $5/second to
the bonus).

For men, the time to beat is the standard of 14:30, and the bonus is
$20/second.
