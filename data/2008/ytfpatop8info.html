Guidelines for Registering for the First Round of the Junior Olympic (PA/JO Top 8) Qualifiers

& Volunteers List


An athlete does not need to pre-qualify to compete in this meet by competing in the Pacific Association Championship (PAC) or any other meet.

Deadline to register for the first round of the Junior Olympic (PA/JO Top 8) qualifiers is Friday (June 13) at midnight.  All registration must be completed via Club Manager and e-mailed to Anthony Camargo at pa.youthentries@yahoo.com.  Be sure that your Club Manager file includes the following:

  • Athlete's full legal name (no  nicknames)
  • Athlete's birthday
  • Athlete's USATF number
  • Athlete's events and best times / marks for each  event (necessary to properly seed heats)
Registration received after the deadline will be charged a $10 per event late fee.  A $20 per event late fee will be charged for athletes who register at the meet.

Every club is requested to also work at the meet.  See the volunteer list to determine where a gap still occurs.  Contact Debra Harvey at pa.youth_vicechair@yahoo.com to sign up.  If you are certified as a USATF official please contact Maria Castillo at pa.youth_officialschair@yahoo.com as the goal is to have one certified official at each field event venue.

When you arrive at pre-registration check-in please ensure that you have the following with you:
  • Clubs
    • Only approved LiveScan coaches can pick up a club  packet
Athlete's birth certificate and Junior Olympic  3-part advancement form sorted in  alphabetical order by last name

 
Copy of your club membership from the PA/USATF  membership database

 
Copy of your birth certificate  membership from Tony Williams website at http://www.leaguelineup.com/judgetony

Unattached Athletes
  • A parent or athlete can pick up the bib  number
  • Birth certificate, Junior Olympic 3-part  advancement form and either a copy of your online registration validation or  your USATF 2008 membership card
Relay declaration forms
  • Only clubs can compete in a  relay
  • Turn in your relay declaration forms at the time you  pick up your bib numbers
  • Write down the name of every appropriate age group  athlete on the form
  • One relay declaration form per relay, per age group,  per sex
Still have questions?  Contact either Maria Castillo at pa.youth_officialschair@yahoo.com or Maura Kent at pa.youth_communicationschair@yahoo.com.
