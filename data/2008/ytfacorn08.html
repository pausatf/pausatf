Acorn/Oscar Bailey All-Comer

Saturday, March 8, 2008 at Berkeley High School

From Darrell Hampton

The meet was incredible. We had about two hundred children participate in races from 100m to 1500m. Throw in some Shot Put and long jumping, that gets you ready for the Grand Finale, the 4x100's. Teams from Sub-Bantam  to Open provided some quality stick work to get us excited about the East Palo Alto Grayhound meet the following week.
 
The Berkeley High School track team did an excellent job keeping the meet running smooth, and the Parents of the Acorn/Oscar Bailey track club did an excellent job with getting the awards out in a timely fashion. The meet started at exactly 10:00 and concluded at 2:15 just as promised. "It doesn't take all Day" to do this Relay. Hope to see you next year.
