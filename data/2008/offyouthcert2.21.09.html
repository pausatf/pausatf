Youth Officials Certification / Recertification / Introduction to Umpiring - Feb 21, 2009

Sign-up today!

This is a special officiating clinic geared toward anyone who would like to work at a youth, high school or college cross country or track and field meet.

If you are 13 years old or older, take this opportunity to be certified at a reduced rate as a USATF official.  This clinic is specifically geared for anyone who will be workinig a college, high school or youth meet.

Certification

  • Date:  Saturday, February 21
  • Time:  9:00 am - 4:30 pm
  • Location:  Oakmont High School
  • Cost:  $20 (this will include your officials training, testing and a USATF officials rule book; you can also purchase additional HS and/or college official rule books if you need them - for other officiating roles)

Recertification (you've already been certified in the past)

  • Date:  Saturday, February 21
  • Time:  9:00 am - 12:00 noon
  • Location:  Oakmont High School
  • Cost:  Free
  • Homework: Contact Jim Hume @ jimhume.certchair@sbcglobal.net to obtain the pre-test that must be completed before coming to the clinic.

Register for both clinics at:  http://www.active.com/page/Event_Details.htm?event_id=1655815&assetId=f59bd7b3-fb8e-42e1-a5bf-49c94fadecb4

Also at Oakmont HS on Feb. 21, 1-4 pm

Introduction to Umpiring: Basic Rules and Skills pdf with more information and registration form; $13; no online registration.

You are not limited to certification only at these clinics.  There are several other opportunities (dates / times) that you can also attend if this session doesn’t work for you.
