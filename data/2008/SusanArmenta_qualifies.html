PA/USATF'S ARMENTA QUALIFIES FOR U.S. OLY TRIALS 20km

Susan Armenta, 34, of San Jose, Calif. is on the comeback trail leading to the 2008 Olympics. Armenta 
surpassed the 1 hour, 48 minute qualifying time for the 2008 U.S. Olympic Team Trials 20K Race Walk 
with a 1:45:42 at an IAAF Challenge Race Walk in Chihuahua, Mexico on March 8, 2008.

Armenta holds the U.S. women's 50km race walk record of 4:39:39, which she set in 2002.
