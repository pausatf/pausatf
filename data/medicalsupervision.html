(updated 7/31)


MEDICAL SUPERVISION
As part of the approval process for obtaining an event sanction, the Pacific Association requires that you have ONE OF THE FOLLOWING AS YOUR PRIMARY MEDICAL PROVIDER, AND THE PROVIDER MUST BE ON SITE OF THE EVENT AT ALL TIMES: EMT (BASIC/PARAMEDIC); NURSE (RN OR LVN); DOCTOR (MD OR DO). We need to identify this person and how to contact this person with current PHONE AND EMAIL. You must include this information on the sanction form under the Medical area.

APPROVED MEDICAL PROVIDERS( PRIMARY PROVIDER:
  • EMT (BASIC / PARAMEDIC) with their own malpractice insurance
  • NURSE (RN OR LVN) with their own malpractice insurance
  • DOCTOR (MD OR DO) with their own malpractice insurance

NOT APPROVED AS PRIMARY MEDICAL PROVIDER (SECONDARY PROVIDER OK) with their own malpractice insurance. THIS PERSON MUST BE AT THE SAME LOCATION AS THE APPROVED PROVIDER.
  • ATHLETIC TRAINER
  • CHIROPRACTOR
  • FIRST AID (BASIC/ ADVANCED)
  • FIRST RESPONDERS
  • EMR (EMERGENCY MEDICAL RESPONDER)
  • PARK RANGERS

For questions or assistance, please contact the Sports Medicine Committee Chair at either 415-722-8876 or specialmed1@yahoo.com.
