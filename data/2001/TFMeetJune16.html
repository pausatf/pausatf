Pacific Association Championships


Sacramento City College


  
Friday 06/15/01 to Saturday 06/16/01             


The 2001 PA USATF Track and Field Championships were
held at Hughes Stadium in Sacramento, Saturday, June 16,
2001.  With this being just five days before The USATF
National Championships in Eugene and the last day to qualify,
there were five US Olympians and one Australian Olympian
getting a final tune up for the big meets this summer.  Nova
Peris(AUS), Sheila Hudson, Suzy Powell, Erica Wheeler,
Nick Hysong,and Andy Bloom, along with many PA 
Open and Masters athletes braved the hot sun to get ready for
the meets to come.
