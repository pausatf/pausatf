USATF Coaches Education Upcoming Schools and Summits
updated Sept 2005

USATF Coaches Education is dedicated to increasing the knowledge and effectiveness of coaches of all levels throughout the United States.  Over 21,000 coaches have taken advantage of these all encompassing learning opportunities. 

For more information and updates, also refer to: http://www.usatf.org/groups/Coaches/education/, or contact pacific association coaches Chair Dave Shrock at: dshrock@pacbell.net
