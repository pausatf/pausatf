Tamalpa Open Men's Cross Country Race Notice

As those at the race know, the open men's race at the John Lawson Tamalpa
Challenge 11/1 was marred by a significant portion of the runners running
off course, most of those eventually running a course around two minutes
longer. Because so many of the finishing times and places were altered by
this occurrence, the race referee decided to invalidate the results of the
race, thus treating the race as if it had not happened.

This might lead teams to believe that they need not submit scoresheets for
this race. Because of possible protests and because of possible LDR
Committee intervention, teams should still submit scoresheets. Please do
so by the usual Wednesday deadline (11/5).

If anyone wishes to protest these results, they should submit their
protest along with their reasoning and any facts they can muster to race
referee Bob Shore, ashorsr@aol.com, by midnight Wednesday, 11/5.

The LDR Committee will determine what to do about the grand prix standings
at its meeting after the Clarksburg 30K on Sunday 11/9.

Thank you all for your understanding.

Tyler Abbott, men's LDR chair
