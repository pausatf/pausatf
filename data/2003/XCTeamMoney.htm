At each race, the race director is responsible for mailing the INDIVIDUAL money amounts to the winners.
But the TEAM amounts are mailed to the PA office. At season end, the PA office mails the sum of the amounts won
to each team. The breakdown below summarizes by race and by team these amounts. The amounts are posted during
the season under the Results link for each race, and the list below merely summarizes what is already posted.
