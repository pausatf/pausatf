2003 National Junior Olympics
Cross Country Championships

Saturday, December 13th
20 miles north of Albuquerque, New Mexico,
at 5500 feet above sea level near the Rio Grande River.

The sun was shining, the temperature was in the high 30s/low 40s with occasional 
heavy gusts of wind. The course was just about 100% grass with some very slight 
'inclines'.  If the times seem slow, remember the high altitude.

Of the 2153 finishers 170 ran for the Pacific Association (7.9%) and 26 ran for the 
Central California Association (1.2%).  Of the 250 individual medals (top 25 in each 
of the 10 age groups) 31 were awarded to PA runners (12.4%), one was for first place.  
Of the 30 team medals (top 3 in each of the 10 age groups) 5 were awarded to PA 
teams (16.6%), 3 were for first place.
