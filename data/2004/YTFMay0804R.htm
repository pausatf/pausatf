May 10, 2004



TO:		Coaches, Parents and Athletes
		Pacific Association USATF

SUBJECT:	Subbantam Championships


Our first Pacific Association Subbantam Championships was contested this past weekend at Mt. Pleasant High 
School in San Jose.  The weather was gorgeous, the crowd was wonderful and the little ones were just as cute 
as they could be.  I truly enjoyed myself and hope you did as well.  I have received feedback from some of you 
to continue this for upcoming years.  I do plan to do just that with your support.  So here’s looking forward to 
a better time next year!!

Thanks again to everyone who supported this event.



Joanne Slaton-Camargo
Youth Chair Pacific Association USATF



P.S.  The computer and camera malfunctioned during the Boys 100 meter heats 3 and 7.  We were not able to 
capture those times or pictures.  I know coaches and parents kept hand times for those athletes if you would 
be so kind as to inform me so I can have it for the record.  My email address is amjcamargo@sbcglobal.net or 
Joanne.Camargo@secmail.ousd.k12.ca.us.
