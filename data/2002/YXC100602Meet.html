Awards:  Ribbons to Top Finishers presented after each race

Race Entry Fee:  $5.00/athlete. A copy of athlete’s Birth Certificate is required.  Make checks payable to Pleasanton Heat.  Please be prepared to pay a $5 parking fee to Shadow Cliffs Regional Park.

Instructions:  Please have all athletes complete the ATHLETE’S RELEASE and have the releases available at the meet.  Teams must wear matching tops. The meet is to be governed by USATF Rule 240.  Sub-Bantams may run in the Bantam races.  Other competitors may only compete in their sex/age group.  An athlete’s year of birth shall determine the division in which they compete.  Submission of falsified or altered documents will result in disqualification of the athlete, and if it involves two or more athletes from a club, the entire team will be disqualified.  Protests concerning the status or eligibility of an entrant must be made to the meet coordinator and director.
