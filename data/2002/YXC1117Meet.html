Junior Olympics/Pacific Association
USA Track & Field


YOUTH CROSS COUNTRY CHAMPIONSHIPS
SUNDAY, NOVEMBER 17, 2002


Spring Lake Park - Santa Rosa, California



See below for directions, entry form and free parking pass

The course is grass, dirt, asphalt and rocky with rolling hills.  Spikes are not recommended.  
The first 25 finishers in each age group will qualify for the Region 14 meet next week at Pleasanton.  
There will be medals to the first 10 finishers.  
The first 3 teams (minimum 5 runners, maximum 8 runners) will qualify for the Region 14 meet.  
Each winning team will receive a plaque.  Athletes must compete in their age group.

       Bantam Girls/Bantam Boys                      Born 1992 or later            3000 meters
       Midget Girls/ Midget Boys                     Born 1990 or 1991             3000 meters
       Youth Girls/Youth Boys                        Born 1988 or 1989             4000 meters
       Intermediate Girls/Intermediate Boys          Born 1986 or 1987             5000 meters
       Young Women/Young Men                         Born 1994 or 1995             5000 meters

The races will be run in the order above.  Some races may be combined (depending on the number of runners) 
but the results and awards will be determined by sex and age group.

Each runner must fill out a separate entry form (make copies of the attached entry form). 
The entry fee is $5.00 per runner and must be turned in with the entry form.  
All runners must have: 1) a USATF card (which may be purchased for $20.00 before 9:00) 
and 2) must have a copy of his/her birth certificate. Each runner will receive a sticker, which 
must be placed on the front of the shirt.




TIME SCHEDULE
Turn in Entry Forms/Purchase USATF Cards.....8:00 to 9:00
Course Walk.....9:00 to 10:00
First Race (Bantam Girls).....10:00
The next race starts shortly after the last runner in the previous race crosses the finish line
