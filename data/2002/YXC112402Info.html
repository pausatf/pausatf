Awards: Medals awarded to top 20 runners in each race.  Winning teams will receive a plaque.

Race Entry Fee: $5.00   Each runner must complete official entry form.  Every runner must have a valid USATF card and a copy of his/her birth certificate.  Make checks payable to Pleasanton Heat.  USATF cards will be available for purchase only to high school athletes who competed, or will compete, for their high school after November 17th.  Please be prepared to pay a $5 parking fee to Shadow Cliffs Regional Park.

Click here for Release Form

Instructions:  Please have all athletes complete the ATHLETE’S RELEASE and have the releases available at the meet.  Teams must wear matching tops. The meet is to be governed by USATF Rule 240.  Runners may only compete in their sex/age group.  An athlete’s year of birth shall determine the division in which they compete.  Submission of falsified or altered documents will result in disqualification of the athlete, and if it involves two or more athletes from a club, the entire team will be disqualified.  Protests concerning the status or eligibility of an entrant must be made to the meet coordinator and director.

NOTE:  The top 20 runners and top 3 teams from each age division will qualify for the Cross Country National Junior Olympic meet.  Entry forms for the National JO meet must be completed and turned in, with a copy of your birth certificate and $6 cash, before you leave Shadow Cliffs Park on Nov. 24.  Be sure to bring your USATF card so you can fill in the correct numbers.
