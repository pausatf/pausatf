Youth Track & Field TV Exposure on Disney Channel
 
Subject: Track and Field Youth Needed for the Disney Channel's Get'cha Head in the Game
 
Dear Association Youth Chairs:
 
A Disney Channel affiliate has asked USATF to help them find two (2) athletes for Disney Channel's Get'cha Head in the Game. If you know of an youth track & field athlete that has a positive attitude and a high degree of passion and love for the sport, then he/she could be the athlete we are looking for!
 
What is Get'cha Head in the Game?
Get'cha Head in the Game is a series of two-minute documentary profiles feature kids approximately 8 - 14 years old who are dedicated and passionate about  a particular sport. The videos run between Disney shows, usually on Saturday and Sunday mornings.  Here is a link to see examples of some of the episodes.
 
Type of athlete we are looking for:
We are looking for  athletes who stands out in the sport of Track & Field, who have a positive attitude, and demonstrate a high degree of passion and love for the sport.  He/she doesn't  necessarily need to be a top ranked competitor, but they must possess a great work ethic and derive tremendous joy from participating in Track & Field. 

What do you as a Youth Chair need to do to "nominate" an athlete? 
If you have an athlete you feel meets the criteria please sendhis/her name and any contact information (phone, e-mail, parents names) by June 9 at 1:00 p.m. EDT (sorry for the quick turnaround) to Halley.Odegaard@usatf.org(USATF intern).  We will then follow-up with the athlete by sending them a questionnaire to determine if they are the right fit.
